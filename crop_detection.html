AI-Based Crop Prediction System

Introduction: An AI-powered system uses Machine Learning (ML) models to predict the best crop to be harvested based on weather conditions, rainfall, market price trends, and historical yield data. By leveraging supervised learning, the system provides data-driven recommendations to maximize agricultural productivity and profitability.

Features:

  • Machine Learning-based crop prediction
  • Real-time weather and market analysis
  • Historical data comparison for accuracy
  • Helps optimize yield and maximize profit
  • Cloud-based insights for farmers

Working Principle: The system collects weather data, rainfall history, and market prices, feeding it into an ML model to recommend the most profitable crop for harvesting.
