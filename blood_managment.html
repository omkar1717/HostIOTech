Blood Managment System

Introduction: To streamline the process of blood donation and management, ensuring that blood is available for those in need.

Features:

  • Show available blood groups
  • Adding blood group into blood-bank
  • Removing blood from blood-bank
  • User authentication

Working Principle: Allows blood donors to register, and individuals in need of blood to check availability. It also manages donor information and blood inventory.
