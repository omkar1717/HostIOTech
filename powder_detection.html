Powder Spread Detection

Introduction: This system uses image processing techniques to analyze the spread of white powder on a black surface. A camera is positioned parallel to the funnel, capturing real-time images to measure the radius of the powder pile.

Features:

  • Real-time image acquisition and analysis
  • OpenCV-based detection and measurement
  • Automated radius calculation for accuracy
  • Fixed camera setup for consistent results
  • Potential applications in material science and quality control

Working Principle: The camera captures images as powder is poured from a funnel onto a black surface. OpenCV processes the images, detects the white powder area, and calculates the spread radius. This data helps in understanding material distribution and optimizing dispensing methods.
