Vehicle Accident Detection and Alert System

Introduction: The Vehicle Accident Detection and Alert System is designed to detect road accidents automatically and send alerts to emergency services and contacts. It utilizes sensors and GPS technology to identify crashes and determine location.

Features:

  • Real-time accident detection using sensors
  • Automatic emergency alert with GPS location
  • Works with GSM for instant message notifications
  • Low power consumption and easy integration with vehicles
  • Improves response time of emergency services

Working Principle: The system uses accelerometers and impact sensors to detect sudden changes in speed or orientation. If an accident is detected, the system sends an alert message with GPS coordinates to emergency contacts or rescue services, ensuring a quick response.
