Wireless Patient Health Monitoring System

Introduction: The Wireless Patient Health Monitoring System is designed to remotely track vital health parameters such as heart rate, blood pressure, temperature, and oxygen levels. This system is crucial for real-time health monitoring and emergency alerts.

Features:

  • Wireless real-time monitoring of vital signs
  • Integration with IoT for remote access
  • Emergency alert system for critical conditions
  • Data logging for patient history analysis
  • Energy-efficient and compact design

Working Principle: The system consists of sensors placed on the patient’s body to collect health data. This data is transmitted wirelessly to a cloud-based platform or hospital monitoring system, where doctors and caregivers can analyze real-time health conditions and respond to emergencies promptly.
