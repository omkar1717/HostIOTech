Smart Water Meter & Water Quality Analysis Using IoT

Introduction: A smart water management system that monitors water quality parameters such as pH, turbidity, TDS, and temperature while also tracking water consumption in real time. Sensor data is sent to the cloud, where it can be analyzed for contamination detection and efficient water usage monitoring. The system provides alerts for unsafe water conditions, promotes water conservation, and enables automated billing or leak detection.

Features:

  • IoT-based remote water monitoring
  • Real-time water quality analysis
  • Automated alerts for contamination
  • Smart water meter for consumption tracking
  • Helps in water conservation efforts

Working Principle: Sensors collect water quality and usage data, transmitting it to a cloud platform for analysis and alerts.
