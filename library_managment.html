Library Managment System

Introduction: Library Management Systems are used to manage information about contents in a library.

Features:

  • Show available books
  • Adding books into library
  • Removing books from library
  • User authentication

Working Principle: Used to manage information relating to books, their names, codes, author names, whether they have been issued or not and if so, who has issued them and what their card’s ID is.
