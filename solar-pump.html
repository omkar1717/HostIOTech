Solar-Powered Water Pumping System

Introduction: The Solar-Powered Water Pumping System is an eco-friendly solution for irrigation and water supply. It harnesses solar energy to pump water from wells, rivers, or reservoirs, making it ideal for remote areas.

Features:

  • Uses solar energy, reducing electricity costs
  • Automatic operation with sensor-based control
  • Suitable for agricultural and domestic use
  • Works efficiently in rural and off-grid locations
  • Low maintenance and long-lasting system

Working Principle: Solar panels convert sunlight into electricity, which powers the pump motor. Water is drawn from a source and supplied to fields or homes. A control system manages flow and optimizes energy use for maximum efficiency.
