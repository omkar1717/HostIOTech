CNC Drilling & Tracking Machine

Introduction: A custom-built CNC machine designed for engraving and drilling on various surfaces like wood, acrylic, and metal. The system operates using stepper motors for precise movement along the X, Y, and Z axes, controlled by a microcontroller. Additionally, a laser module is integrated for etching/drawing images onto the surface. The machine is ideal for engraving patterns, text, and custom designs, making it suitable for DIY, artistic, and industrial applications.

Features:

  • CNC-controlled precision engraving and drilling
  • Laser module for detailed artwork
  • Supports multiple materials
  • Automated operation with G-code compatibility
  • Customizable design patterns

Working Principle: The CNC system follows a programmed path to engrave or drill based on user-defined designs. A stepper motor-driven mechanism ensures accuracy, while the laser module burns detailed patterns on surfaces.
