Automated Railway Gate Control System

Introduction: The Automated Railway Gate Control System is designed to enhance railway safety by automating the opening and closing of gates at level crossings. It minimizes human errors, prevents accidents, and improves train schedule efficiency.

Features:

  • Automatic detection of approaching trains using sensors
  • Automated gate control to ensure road safety
  • LED indicators and alarms for warning signals
  • Remote monitoring and control through IoT integration
  • Low maintenance and energy-efficient system

Working Principle: The system utilizes sensors placed at a fixed distance from the railway crossing. When a train is detected, signals are sent to a microcontroller, which activates the gate mechanism. Once the train passes, the gates automatically reopen for road traffic.
